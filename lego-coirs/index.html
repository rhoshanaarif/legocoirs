Product Description

coco peat discs

Coco Peat Discs, Coir pith Discs, Coco Planks. Vaighai Coco Discs are compressed Coir Pith made for use in pots. Coco Discs are also made with a hole in the middle to facilitate easy planting. these discs are called planks.


coir-pith-block

Coir pith block is a compressed material of coir pith, which can be used for plantation. It can be used as a supplement for soil. Few properties of coir pith block make it viable for plantation. 100% biodegradable: - Pith blocks are biodegradable and used as an organic nutrient to the plantation.


coconut fibre bales

We are widely renowned as the leading manufacturer and supplier of high quality coconut husk fiber that is extracted from matured green and brown coconut husks. This is available in two type brown and white. Brown fiber is used for fabrication of doormats, brushes, mattresses and other upholstery material and white fiber is used for making ropes.


grow bags

A growbag is a large plastic bag filled with a growing medium and used for growing plants, usually tomatoes or other salad crops. ... Planting is undertaken by first laying the bag flat on the floor or bench of the growing area, then cutting access holes in the uppermost surface, into which the plants are inserted.


500-650 gm bricks

Cocopeat is a fiber form derived from coconut husks. Cocopeat is washed dried & compressed into 5 kg blocks for your convenience. Cocopeat has a unique ability to withstand high compaction force without leaving its beneficial structure. Hydrate the blocks slowly by using 25 liters of water and allow the moisture to be fully absorbed. Once the block is completely reconstituted to get the yield 70 – 75 liters. Now it’s ready to improve your potting soils or use as a 100% soil less growing medium. It is a perfect growing media for flower and vegetable cultivation. Throughout this entire process, samples will be taken and tested by in-house laboratories to ensure that each batch complies with the required specifications.


coco chips

Coconut meat is the rich white lining that is contained within the shell of a coconut. Coconut meat can be juicy and tender, or slightly thick and crunchy, to tough and fibrous depending on how long the kernel has been stored. Coconut Chips.


coconut copra

Copra, dried sections of the meat of the coconut, the kernel of the fruit of the coconut palm (Cocos nucifera). Copra is valued for the coconut oil extracted from it and for the resulting residue, coconut-oil cake, which is used mostly for livestock feed. ... About 30 nuts provide meat for 10 pounds (4.5 kg) of copra.

coconut shell

A coconut is a large, nut-like fruit that grows high in trees. Coconuts have very hard shells and sweet, white flesh inside. The white, flaked coconut you can buy in a bag at the grocery store is a processed form of the coconut fruit.
